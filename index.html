About Me

I'm a recent graduate of Rochester Institute of Technology with a BS in Imaging Science and a focus in computer vision and machine learning.

At RIT I was a member of the Machine and Neuromorphic Perception Laboratory (kLab) as part of the VQA (Visual Question Answering) team and also the Computational Linguistics and Speech Processing (CLASP) lab.

I love exploring research and applications of deep learning in computer vision, natural language processing, and artificial intelligence. I currently have a paper in review at a top computer vision conference and will find out soon if it is accepted!
